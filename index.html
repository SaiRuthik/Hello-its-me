About

I'm a Robotics & Automation Engineer with hands-on experience across quantum photonics research, industrial automation, and commercial data engineering. My career has taken me through MSc research labs, production automation platforms, and retail tech stacks — the common thread being intelligent systems that replace tedious human effort with precise, repeatable automation.

I believe the most powerful work happens when you understand both ends of the stack — from servo motors to cloud dashboards.
